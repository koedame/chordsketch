Brand

A white quill on a crimson field. Writing and notation.

Wordmark · Noto Sans JP 700

ChordSketch

Crimson appears as a large color field only inside the mark itself. Clearspace ≥ ¼ of mark height, or ≥ 24px next to the wordmark. Minimum 24px (mobile) / 32px (print). The mark sits on white or --ink-50; never on crimson.

Crimson is the only accent. Ink — warm, slightly red — carries everything else.
