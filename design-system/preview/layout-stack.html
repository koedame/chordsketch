Stack

The vertical-flow primitive. Spacing between stacked elements is owned by the container via --stack-gap, never by per-child margin-bottom. Numbered modifiers map 1:1 onto the --sp-* scale; mixed rhythm is expressed by nesting.

Children own no margin

A .stack-4 wrapping native <h3> / <p> with no margin rules of their own. The primitive zeroes margin-block on direct children, so the only vertical spacing is the gap — correct with or without a global reset.
